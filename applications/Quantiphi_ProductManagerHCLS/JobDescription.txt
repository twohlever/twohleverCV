What you’ll do: 


We are looking for a seasoned, passionate and detail-oriented Product Manager to manage the development, maintenance, support, upgrades, and product roadmaps of Quantiphi’s assigned HCLS products. At Quantiphi, you will be working with your leadership team, advisory services, marketing, R&D, Sales, and Marketing teams to oversee the product/solution research, develop strategy, communicate plans, coordinate development and act on feedback and data analysis to ensure products remain current, relevant, and impactful to meet current or anticipated market demand. You will be responsible for maintaining and growing business by managing Quantiphi’s assigned product(s). 

Role & Responsibilities:

Gain expertise about the company and product market, user pe sonas and competitors

Shape the industry knowledge into a high level strategic plan for the product - including goals and objectives, R&D, product overviews, roadmaps and timelines

Develop a working strategic plan using a product roadmap and present it to key stakeholders across the organization including executives, investors, and development teams

Coordinate with relevant teams including marketing, research, development, sales, advisory services to begin executing the plan 

Learn and update strategic and development plans using relevant  data analysis and and solicit direct feedback from users 

Develop trusted relationships across product portfolio of the major client(s) and partners to ensure that they are actively engaged with Quantiphi for product feedback

Develop trusted advisor relationships with key customer stakeholders, partners, and executive sponsors

As a product manager, you are an expert at contributing to different phases of the sales and product development lifecycle 

Acquire a thorough understanding of key customer and partner goals, needs and requirements to enhance product development 

Act as the main point of contact in all matters relating to assigned product(s) 

Help address any issues and/or problems faced by customers and deal with complaints to maintain trust and client satisfaction

Development of sales enablement tools and training, marketing support materials, implementation and training plans, pricing models and ongoing support and maintenance plans 

Communicate proactively with all related team members to identify opportunities/issues, create solutions enhancements, and implement efficiency improvements

Facilitate effective delivery of agile events across software development teams including backlog refinement, iteration planning, daily standups and retrospectives

Work with Engineering, DevOps, QA and Design to facilitate and assist in: Scoping, requirements gathering, backlog management, dependency coordination, capacity planning, sprint and release management

Assist dependent stakeholders in maintaining roadmaps ensuring technical feasibility, capacity/resource allocation, risk analysis, inter-team and department dependencies, go-to-market plans and downstream impacts of changes or delays

Use tools, cadences and communication to effectively create and maintain visibility, track progress, escalate blockers, run sprint reports, collect metrics and drive relentless improvement

BA/BS degree in Business/Marketing, Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics or related technical field, or equivalent practical experience in a healthcare-related field in the development of healthcare-related and/or life sciences or biotech products

5+ years experience working directly with/for healthcare and/or life sciences and/or biotech organizations or with vendors in product management roles
